In this transformation, last three rows of State are cyclically shifted to the left.

As illustration:

--------------    --------------
|d4 e0 b8 1e | -> |d4 e0 b8 1e |
|27 bf b4 41 | -> |bf b4 41 27 |
|11 98 5d 52 | -> |5d 52 11 98 |
|ae f1 e5 30 | -> |30 ae f1 e5 |
--------------    --------------


This is first ShiftRows transformation from FIPS-197, page 33.
You can edit contents of State and play with results.
