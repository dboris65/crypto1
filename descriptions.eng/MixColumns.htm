MixColumns transformation takes column by column from AES State and performs matrix multiplication as follows:

      | 02 03 01 01 |
new = | 01 02 03 01 | * old_column
      | 01 01 02 03 |
      | 03 01 01 02 |
or

|n1|   | 02 03 01 01 |   |o1|
|n2| = | 01 02 03 01 | * |o2|
|n3|   | 01 01 02 03 |   |o3|
|n4|   | 03 01 01 02 |   |o4|
 

To see it in action, just press MixColumns button. Initial values are from FIPS-197, page 33. Moreover, You can play with State values to check results.
