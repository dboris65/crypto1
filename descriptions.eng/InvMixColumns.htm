To reverse MixColumns transformation, InvMixColumns takes column by column from AES State and performs matrix multiplication within Galois field, but with different matrix, as follows:

      | 0e 0b 0d 09 |
new = | 09 0e 0b 0d | * old_column
      | 0d 09 0e 0b |
      | 0b 0d 09 0e |
or

|n1|   | 0e 0b 0d 09 |   |o1|
|n2| = | 09 0e 0b 0d | * |o2|
|n3|   | 0d 09 0e 0b |   |o3|
|n4|   | 0b 0d 09 0e |   |o4|
 

To see it in action, just press InvMixColumns button. Initial values are from FIPS-197, page 37. Moreover, You can play with State values to check results.
